Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I change my dietary preferences?

A: Go to the Profile tab in the app, tap on "Dietary Preferences", and you can update your community (e.g., Jain, Vegan) or add specific allergies.

Q: The scanner didn't recognize a product. What should I do?

A: Ensure you are in a well-lit area and holding the camera steady. If it still fails, you can use the Manual Search feature or report a bug via the Profile settings.

Q: How can I delete my account?

A: You can delete your account directly inside the app under Profile → Delete Account. Alternatively, visit our Privacy Policy page for instructions on how to request deletion via email.

Q: Is my data safe?

A: Yes. We use industry-standard encryption and only share necessary data with our AI partners for analysis. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
