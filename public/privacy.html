Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Promade Solutions Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") operates Food Analyser. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly:

  • Account information: Name, Email Address.
  • Dietary preferences: Community (e.g., Jain, Vegan), exceptions, and allergies.
  • Location: Country/region for regional ingredient rules.
  • User Content: Images of food labels when you scan products.

We also collect automatically:

  • Device information (model, OS version) for bug reports.
  • Usage data (scan history, timestamps).
  • IP address for security purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To analyze food labels based on your specific dietary restrictions.
  • To provide personalized verdicts (SAFE, UNSAFE, CAUTION, UNCERTAIN).
  • To store your scan history for your future reference.
  • To send necessary account emails (password resets, verification).
  • To improve our AI accuracy (using anonymized, aggregated data only).

3. AI Processing Disclosure

Transparency Note: Your scanned images are processed by third-party AI services (Google Gemini and OpenAI) solely to analyze ingredients. These providers process data according to their privacy policies. We do not use your images for training their public AI models.

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Account data: Retained until you delete your account.
  • Scan images: Automatically deleted from our storage after 90 days.
  • Scan history (text): Retained until you delete your account.

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • AI providers (Google, OpenAI): For ingredient analysis.
  • Cloud infrastructure (Supabase, Backblaze): For secure storage.
  • Law enforcement: Only if legally required.

6. Account Deletion

You have full control over your data.

Method 1 (In-App): Go to Profile → Delete Account to permanently remove your data instantly.

Method 2 (Web Request): If you cannot access the app, you may request account deletion by emailing us.

To request deletion via email:
Email: umang@lightningaisolutions.in
Subject: "Account Deletion Request"

Please send the email from the address associated with your account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

7. Children's Privacy

Food Analyser is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via email or an in-app notice.

9. Contact Us
